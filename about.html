About Us

Literature   Review

When dealing with a large number of customers at once, the time-consuming and laborious process of taking orders for meals may be detrimental to the success of an organization.

One way to think about the conventional approaches is as either paper-based or verbally-based. This entails the waiter coming to the table, taking down items that clients have ordered, and passing that paper to the kitchen staff for preparation.

RDA enables us to efficiently manage and serve customers at the earliest and contributes greatly to the success of the business partner.

About our app

RDA Webapp is the result of an idea to solve the common problem of university students who find little time in between their classes to have a snack or a meal to keep them going through the day. It's a convenient platform comprised of six modules from your menu to online payment, all automated for an easy, to-go, snack that'll surely save time.
